# Divergent Universe: Protean Hero Version 4.0 Updates
## Divergent Universe: Protean Hero Version 4.0 Updates
![Banner](https://sdk.hoyoverse.com/upload/ann/2026/01/19/fd33a9817a31870f2f2335a0bd7fa687_206228622078536494_transformed.png)

After the Version 4.0 update, Divergent Universe: Protean Hero will unlock Insight Mode and feature a double Synchronicity Points event!

### New Content

- "Insight Mode" activated

After activating "Insight Mode," all allies' final DMG dealt increases, while DMG taken decreases.

Additionally, you can gain random Blessings and Cosmic Fragments by entering Domains.

Additionally, obtain a random Curio and Golden Blood's Boon when entering the second and third planes.

※ After clearing with "Insight Mode" activated, you can still obtain Divergent Universe Points, Synchronicity Points, titles, and other rewards as normal.

- Double Synchronicity Points Acquisition

After this update, you can gain double Synchronicity Points in Divergent Universe: Protean Hero.

### "Divergent Universe: Protean Hero" Coming to a Close

After the countdown concludes (and Version 4.0 ends), the Divergent Universe: Protean Hero rewards will be reset. The rewards you haven't claimed will become unclaimable, so Trailblazers should take care to get their rewards in time.

### Rewards Reset Details

- After the countdown concludes (and Version 4.0 ends), the Divergent Universe: Protean Hero rewards will be reset. The reset rewards include:

Gallery of Possibilities Rewards and Collection Progress

Stable Computing Array Rewards

Rewards and Levels of Synchronicity Level

Ordinary Extrapolation First-Time Clearance Rewards

※ "Threshold Protocol" progress and the "Divergent Universe: Protean Hero" title will be carried over based on specific rules.

※ Activated Inspiration Circuit Nodes and claimed Inspiration **will not be reset**.

※ After the countdown ends, all Operational Records of Divergent Universe: Protean Hero will be **unlocked**.
