# Version 2.2 New Stages
## Version 2.2 New Stages
![Banner](https://sdk.hoyoverse.com/upload/ann/2024/04/15/afbc481eb2fe0b58db36b432aaa41ea5_7353081101901967369.jpg)

Dear Trailblazers,

After the Version 2.2 update, new stages Echo of War: Salutations of Ashen Dreams, Stagnant Shadow: Shape of Duty, and Bud of The Hunt: SoulGlad™ Scorchsand Audition Venue will be added!

### Echo of War: Salutations of Ashen Dreams

Clear the stage to obtain the new **"Lost Echo of the Shared Wish" Trace Material**, "The Ashblazing Grand Duke" and "Watchmaker, Master of Dream Machinations" **Relics**, "Lucent Afterglow" item, "What Is Real?" and "Dreamville Adventure" Light Cone, etc.

![img](https://sdk.hoyoverse.com/upload/ann/2024/04/23/e11bdb0f38dbe159f7af51d7afb1524e_4025848062409419643.png)

### Stagnant Shadow: Shape of Duty

Clear the stage to obtain the new **Physical-Type** character **Ascension material** "IPC Work Permit."

![img](https://sdk.hoyoverse.com/upload/ann/2024/04/23/8ae80574d941daa920eca57a48b878ae_5557978141603768151.png)

### Bud of The Hunt: SoulGlad™ Scorchsand Audition Venue

Clear the stage to obtain the new **Hunt** character **Trace Material** "Countertemporal Shot," "Destined Expiration," and "Meteoric Bullet."
