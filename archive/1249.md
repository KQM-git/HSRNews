# Birthday Cake Update Details
## Birthday Cake Update Details
![Banner](https://sdk.hoyoverse.com/upload/ann/2026/03/30/0554bbd76a886432913b08575c19e75d_7989548708540086360_transformed.png)

Dear Trailblazers,

As Honkai: Star Rail approaches its three-year anniversary, the birthday cake in the birthday mail will also have an update.

### Birthday Cake Update Details

- After 2026/04/26 00:00:00 (server), the birthday cake in the birthday mail will switch from "Golden Blessing" to "Phantasia's Blessing".

![img](https://sdk.hoyoverse.com/upload/ann/2026/04/10/b152c374a7692292d32d7fd38692abab_1913278351398259412_transformed.png)

- If the Trailblazer's birthday is set for April 27, logging into the game on April 27 will get them the new birthday cake "Phantasia's Blessing".

- If the Trailblazer's birthday is set for April 24, logging into the game on April 24, 2027, will get them the new birthday cake "Phantasia's Blessing".

### Special Note

- If the Trailblazer logged in for the first time on or before their set birthdate, they will receive the birthday mail and cake during that anniversary year (from April 25 to April 24 of the following year).

- If the Trailblazer logged into the game for the first time after their birthdate, they will receive the birthday mail and cake during the next anniversary period (between April 25 of the following year and April 24 of the year after).

- You can claim your birthday mail and cake within 30 days starting from your set birthday. The version of the cake is determined by your set birth date.
