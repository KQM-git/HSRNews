# New Stagnant Shadow and Calyx (Crimson) Stages
## Version 2.1 New Stages
![Banner](https://sdk.hoyoverse.com/upload/ann/2024/03/12/68866258e50ae4fb9f63bf58dd8fb249_7096358593774532227.jpg)

Dear Trailblazers,

After the V2.1 update, Penacony and the Xianzhou Luofu will gain the new stages Stagnant Shadow: Shape of Ire, Bud of Preservation (Clock Studios Theme Park), and Bud of Abundance (Fyxestroll Garden).

### Stagnant Shadow

■ Stagnant Shadow: Shape of Ire

Clear the stage to obtain the **new Fire-Type** character Ascension material "Raging Heart."

![img](https://sdk.hoyoverse.com/upload/ann/2024/03/12/01f77cb454b60dc09da34533fc218455_2171920013558220610.png)

### Calyx (Crimson)

■ Bud of Preservation (Clock Studios Theme Park)

Clear the stage to obtain the brand-new Path of **Preservation** character Trace Materials Divine Amber, Crystal Meteorites, and Scattered Stardust.

![img](https://sdk.hoyoverse.com/upload/ann/2024/03/12/3a6770923e8721e25eeae341bb874616_7127393654654000799.png)

■ Bud of Abundance (Fyxestroll Garden)

Clear the stage to obtain the brand-new Path of **Abundance** character Trace Materials Myriad Fruit, Nourishing Honey, and Alien Tree Seed.

![img](https://sdk.hoyoverse.com/upload/ann/2024/03/12/26acd2cb9e7cfd1513cdab1dd2bb58d1_4478128168255936163.png)
