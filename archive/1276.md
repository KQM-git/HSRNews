# Version 4.2 HoYoLAB Tools Update Overview
## Version 4.2 HoYoLAB Tools Update Overview

Hello, Trailblazers!

Following the arrival of Version 4.2, new content has also been added to HoYoLAB's community tools to help Trailblazers in their cosmic adventures! Come and take a look!

**"Tool Compendium"**

### "Scholar Wubbie: Beta"

- Added team setup and Relic build recommendations for Silver Wolf LV.999 (Elation: Imaginary), Evanescia (Elation: Physical), and Trailblazer (Elation: Lightning).

- Updated the recommendations for the characters Firefly (Destruction: Fire), Huohuo (Abundance: Wind), Seele (The Hunt: Quantum), and Welt (Nihility: Imaginary). Scholar Wubbie will provide build recommendations based on the state of the aforementioned characters after Character Enhancement.

### Leveling Suggestions

- Added recommended setups for Silver Wolf LV.999 (Elation: Imaginary) and Trailblazer (Elation: Lightning). The recommended setup for the character Evanescia (Elation: Physical) will be updated after the "The Demoiselle in Charge" Character Event Warp becomes available.

- Updated the recommended setups for the characters Firefly (Destruction: Fire), Huohuo (Abundance: Wind), Seele (The Hunt: Quantum), and Welt (Nihility: Imaginary). Build recommendations will be provided based on the state of the aforementioned characters after Character Enhancement.

### Material Calculation

- New characters: Silver Wolf LV.999 (Elation: Imaginary), Evanescia (Elation: Physical), and Trailblazer (Elation: Lightning).

- New Light Cones: Welcome to the Cosmic City (Elation), Until the Flowers Bloom Again (Elation), and Tomorrow, Together (Elation).

※ The Leveling Calculator tool will also be updated with information regarding the aforementioned characters and Light Cones.
