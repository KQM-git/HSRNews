# "Holy City Appraisals": Appraise Okhema antiques to obtain Stellar Jades and other rewards.
## "Holy City Appraisals" Event Details
![Banner](https://sdk.hoyoverse.com/upload/ann/2025/02/12/23fb700d30e3eb52872120c17d766c56_6023048766167064044.png)

The Okhema Market has been flooded with fake antiques lately. Theodoros, the antique shop owner, is desperately seeking an apprentice to help authenticate items...

### Event Period

2025/03/19 12:00:00 (server) – 2025/04/07 03:59:00 (server)

### Participation Requirements

Trailblaze Level ≥ 21

※ This content can be experienced in advance through the "Finality's Vision" function.

※ It is recommended that Trailblazers experience this content after completing the Trailblaze Mission "Amphoreus" - "Distant Travelers, Listen to this World's Prayer".

### Event Rewards

![img](https://sdk.hoyoverse.com/upload/ann/2025/02/17/586fe68b945dd1f8a637e27c205ef530_6479619640779984973.png)

### Event Details

- After the event starts, Theodoros will everyday reveal a new **"Unidentified Treasure"** and the **"Appraisal Consumables"** needed to assist in its identification, with **a total of 7 treasures**.

- After collecting and submitting the required **"Appraisal Consumables"**, Trailblazers can obtain the day's **appraisal rewards**.
