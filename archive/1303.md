# Version 4.3 New Stages
## Version 4.3 New Stages
![Banner](https://sdk.hoyoverse.com/upload/ann/2026/05/09/b50230cb15d307645b21a22346a71bf0_1431103054595234634_transformed.png)

Dear Trailblazers,

After the Version 4.3 update, new stages will be added: Cavern of Corrosion: Path of Insight, Stagnant Shadow: Shape of Devour, Bud of Erudition: Seafeld TV Tower, and Bud of Destruction: Inkford Hermitage.

### Cavern of Corrosion: Path of Insight

Successfully challenge the stage "Cavern of Corrosion: Path of Insight" to obtain the new **Cavern Relics** "As Navigator Isee Sees It", "Divine-Querying Master Smith", and more.

![img](https://sdk.hoyoverse.com/upload/ann/2026/05/22/0c844919981d46e8195363472576a898_6464653442656253394_transformed.png)

New Cavern Relic Effects Preview:

- **"As Navigator Isee Sees It"**

2-Pc Set: Increases ATK by 12%.

4-Pc Set: When the wearer enters combat or uses Skill, the DMG dealt by their Skill and Ultimate increases by 18%, stacking up to 3 times. At the start of the wearer's turn or after using Ultimate, removes 1 stack of this effect.

- **"Divine-Querying Master Smith"**

2-Pc Set: Increases Max HP by 12%.

4-Pc Set: Increases the wearer's CRIT DMG dealt to enemy targets in the DEF reduction state by 28%. After the wearer inflicts the DEF reduction state on an enemy target, all allies gain "Comburent" for 2 turns. This effect cannot be stacked. DMG dealt by ally targets with "Comburent" increases by 15%. This effect can be triggered again after the wearer uses an attack.
