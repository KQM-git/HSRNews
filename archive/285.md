Hello, Trailblazers

The Version 1.2 "Even Immortality Ends" Strategy Guides Contest Event has begun! Submit your work to win Stellar Jades!

[ Go to the event](https://hoyo.link/0fDWDDAd)

### How to Participate

During the event, if you submit a **strategy guide** on the event page that meets the event requirements, you will be deemed to have successfully participated.

In this round of the strategy guide competition, works relating to **new events** (Simulated Universe and limited-time events) and **characters** (Blade, Luka, or Kafka), with "StarRail1.2" in the title, and posted with the topics #gameguides and #HonkaiStarRail will be more popular!.

### Event Period

Submission Period: 07/19/2023 – 23:59, 08/29/2023 (UTC+8)

Selection Period: 08/29/2023 – 13:59, 09/15/2023 (UTC+8)

Result Announcement: 09/18/2023 – 14:00 (UTC+8)
