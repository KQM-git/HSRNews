# Version 1.6 HoYoLAB Tools Update
## Version 1.6 HoYoLAB Tools Update
![Banner](https://sdk.hoyoverse.com/upload/ann/2023/12/21/0d1ebad1a62a1a81ebb684b7356239f0_1682042805771448331.jpg)

Following the arrival of Version 1.6, new content has also accompanied the update for HoYoLAB's community tools to help Trailblazers across their cosmic adventures! Come and take a look!

### Honkai: Star Rail Map

- Map information for Seclusion Zone has been added to help Trailblazers quickly explore and collect content in the new version.

[ Click to go to Honkai: Star Rail Map](https://hoyo.link/71mwCBAd)

### Battle Records

- The Battle Records tool has included the "Critter Pick" and "Virtual Scentventure" event modules and will add the "Pure Fiction" and "Memory of Chaos" Stages 11–12. Trailblazers will be able to check their event completion progress in the corresponding module.

- The Battle Records widget is estimated to add the character backgrounds of "Ruan Mei (Harmony: Ice)," "Dr. Ratio (The Hunt: Imaginary)," and "Xueyi (Destruction: Quantum)" on the first part January 2024.

[ Click to go to Battle Records](https://hoyo.link/3bnwCBAd)

### Leveling Calculator

- Adds leveling materials related to "Ruan Mei (Harmony: Ice)," "Dr. Ratio (The Hunt: Imaginary)," and "Xueyi (Destruction: Quantum)." Trailblazers who want to start collecting new character leveling materials early can use this to help your advantage!

- Adds the ability to extract information on material quantities in the Inventory. Trailblazers can now calculate the remaining required amount of materials based on those in the Inventory.

[ Click to head to the Leveling Calculator](https://hoyo.link/3dpwCBAd)

### HoYoWiki

- Adds the character information of "Ruan Mei (Harmony: Ice)," "Dr. Ratio (The Hunt: Imaginary)," and "Xueyi (Destruction: Quantum)."

- Adds the Interastral Peace Corporation entry.

※ We are also working hard to set up more V1.6 information. Stay tuned!

[ Click to go to HoYoWiki](https://hoyo.link/c5lwCBAd)

### Lineup Assistant

- Adds the characters "Ruan Mei (Harmony: Ice)," "Dr. Ratio (The Hunt: Imaginary)," and "Xueyi (Destruction: Quantum)."

- Adds content about Pure Fiction and Memory of Chaos stages 11–12.
