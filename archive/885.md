## "Through the Petals in the Land of Repose" Mission Details
![Banner](https://sdk.hoyoverse.com/upload/ann/2025/03/18/3b04fc60b8aca736c1470d3b36b0ddbc_5163398969002401133.png)

Those from the past rush by in a fleeting lifetime, those from the future stretch into an endless night — oh, lost wanderer, cross that uncharted Sea of Souls, and step into the blooming fields of Antila flowers.

### Availability

Permanently available after the Version 3.2 update

### Unlock Requirement

Complete the Trailblaze Mission "Amphoreus — Passage, Reveal the Past Once More"

※ Amphoreus Trailblaze Missions can be experienced in advance through the "Finality's Vision: Leap of Trailblaze" function.
