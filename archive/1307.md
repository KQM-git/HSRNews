### Update and Compensation Details

Version 4.3 will run from after the Version 4.3 update until 2026/07/15 06:00:00 (global).

■ Update Time

Begins at 2026/06/01 06:00:00 (global). The update will take approximately 5 hours.

■ Compensation Details

- Server Maintenance Compensation

Compensation: Stellar Jade ×300

Eligible Recipients: Trailblazers with Trailblaze Level ≥ 4 before 2026/06/01 06:00:00 (global)

※ Please claim before the end of Version 4.3.

- Bug Compensation

Compensation: Stellar Jade ×300

Eligible Recipients: Trailblazers with Trailblaze Level ≥ 4 before 2026/06/01 06:00:00 (global)

※ Please log in to claim the mail before 2026/07/01 23:59:00 (global).

The Crew will issue the compensation via in-game mail to Trailblazers within 5 hours after the update is complete. Be sure to claim it in time.

■ How to Update

- PC: Exit the game, restart the launcher, then click "Update"

- Android: Enter the game and follow the instructions in the in-game pop-up window to complete the update

- iOS: Enter the App Store and tap "Update"

- PS5®: After entering the game, the game will automatically download and install the update

### Version Update Details

1. New Story

**■ Trailblaze Mission "Planarcadia" - "The Lethe Below the Living"**

- A passerby seeking a final resting place, a traveler bearing admonitions, people journey to the end of the painting where the ancient Lethe flows, stand witness to past joys and sorrows that emerge from within...

- Availability: Permanently available after the Version 4.3 update

- Participation Requirement: Complete Trailblaze Mission "Planarcadia" - "So Laughed the Masses"

※ "Planarcadia" Trailblaze Missions can be experienced in advance through the "Finality's Vision: Leap of Trailblaze" function.

2. New Character

**■ 5-Star character Mortenax Blade (Nihility: Fire)**

Mortenax Blade is a damage-dealing character who deploys a special Zone to gain enhancements and brand-new abilities. While the Zone is active, ally targets can frequently inflict DEF-reducing and Vulnerability debuffs on enemies, and also trigger Mortenax Blade's additional Skill Follow-Up ATK.

3. New Light Cone

**■ 5-Star Reforged in Hellfire (Nihility)**

Obtainable through the "Brilliant Fixation: Reforged in Hellfire" Light Cone Event Warp.

4. New Outfit

**■ Trailblaze Outfit - Back "Karmic Fire of the Distant Shore"**

Can be obtained via "Gift of Tempered Blade."

The brand-new Trailblaze Outfit part "Back" can be equipped simultaneously with other parts (such as Headwear and Outfits).

5. New Area

**■ Inkford Hermitage**

The boundary between the World in Canvas and reality. From here, the imagenae bid farewell to the mortal world and retreat into the canvas. After sorting through centuries of experiences and stories, they settle all worldly ties.

6. New Events

**■ Pixel Plane Rumble**

- A classic game returns to the arcadian skies as pixel planes set sail once again. In a fierce dogfight between ninety-nine fighters, only the final victor can claim air superiority... Customize your ultimate aircraft, devise a winning strategy, and break through the bullet hell!

- Event Period: After the Version 4.3 update – 2026/07/15 03:59:00 (global)

- Requirement: Trailblaze Level ≥ 21

※ This content can be experienced in advance through the Finality's Vision function.

※ It is recommended that Trailblazers complete the Trailblaze Mission "Planarcadia — Unraveled for Daybreak" before experiencing this content.

**■ Wispae Amusement Park**

- Trendy styles change in the blink of an eye, bringing an opportunity for Star Rail FEST's renewal. Plan out the routes, cleverly arrange the facilities, and build an exclusive arcadia for the wispae in the World in Canvas! Your creative concepts will serve as the perfect inspiration for optimizing the Exhibition Area.

- Event Period: 2026/06/24 12:00:00 (server) – 2026/07/15 03:59:00 (global)

- Requirement: Trailblaze Level ≥ 21

※ This content can be experienced in advance through the Finality's Vision function.

※ It is recommended that Trailblazers complete the Trailblaze Mission "Planarcadia — Unraveled for Daybreak" before experiencing this content.

**■ Gift of Tempered Blade**

- During the event, after obtaining "Mortenax Blade" via Warps, Trailblazers can claim the Trailblazer Outfit - Back "Karmic Fire of the Distant Shore" from the "Gift of Tempered Blade" or "Now I Am Become Blade" Character Event Warp screen.

- Event Period: After the Version 4.3 update – 2026/06/24 11:59:00 (server)

- Participation requirement: Warp to obtain the limited 5-star character Mortenax Blade (Nihility: Fire)

**■ Gift of Odyssey**

Log in every day during the event to obtain check-in rewards. Trailblazers can claim Star Rail Special Pass ×10 after checking in for 7 days!

※ Please refer to future announcements for information on other events.

- Apocalyptic Shadow: Gale of Forgetting

2026/06/08 04:00:00 (server) – 2026/07/20 03:59:00 (server)

2026/06/22 04:00:00 (server) – 2026/08/03 03:59:00 (server)

When an ally target inflicts a debuff on an enemy target, allies additionally accumulate 1 Grit Value. This effect can be triggered up to 10 times per enemy target.

2026/07/06 04:00:00 (server) – 2026/08/17 03:59:00 (server)

Increases Ultimate and Follow-Up ATK DMG dealt by ally targets by 50%. After an ally character uses their Ultimate or Follow-Up ATK, adds 1 Hit Per Action to "Memory Turbulence," up to a maximum of 15 hits.

At the start of each Cycle, "Memory Turbulence" deals 1 instance of True DMG to a random enemy target with each Hit Per Action.

After clearing Starward Mode in each gameplay and obtaining the Prismatic Star, you can get additional rewards such as Stellar Jades. Please refer to the in-game descriptions for details.
