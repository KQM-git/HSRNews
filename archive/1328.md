# Version 4.4 New Stages
## Version 4.4 New Stages
![Banner](https://sdk.hoyoverse.com/upload/ann/2026/06/13/ed3f19e1a6446fa047d70b5e49cfb795_1017083365265037342_transformed.png)

Dear Trailblazers,

After the Version 4.4 update, the new stages Echo of War: The Comedy of Doom and Divergent Universe: Bugs Incoming will be added.

### Echo of War: The Comedy of Doom

Successfully challenge the stage to obtain the new **Trace Material** "High Hopes of the Falsely Enlightened," **Relics** "Ever-Glorious Magical Girl" and "Self-Enshrouded Recluse," the item "Lucent Afterglow," Light Cones "Mushy Shroomy's Adventures" and "Victory In a Blink," and more.

![img](https://sdk.hoyoverse.com/upload/ann/2026/06/24/13caa4054b22ceade008d095f3efbb91_7957582568667997104_transformed.png)

### Planar Ornament Extraction

■ Divergent Universe: Bugs Incoming

Successfully challenge the stage "Divergent Universe: Bugs Incoming," and Trailblazers can use Trailblaze Power, Immersifiers, or Deep Immersifiers to obtain the brand-new Planar Ornaments **Fallen Star Anchorage and Cosmic Life Sciences Institute** via "Planar Ornament Extraction."

![img](https://sdk.hoyoverse.com/upload/ann/2026/06/24/f43dfe9dc24ce389eebecf216a7b4c73_1882444955675193413_transformed.png)

New Planar Ornament Effects Preview:

- **Fallen Star Anchorage**

Link Rope: Silver Rails of the Anchorage

Planar Sphere: Stranded Express at the Anchorage

2-Pc: Increases the wearer's CRIT Rate by 8%. When entering combat, if the wearer and another teammate are both Trailblaze Companions characters, increases the wearer's CRIT DMG by 32%.

- **Cosmic Life Sciences Institute**

Link Rope: Peripheral Conduits of the Life Sciences Institute

Planar Sphere: Central Synapse of the Life Sciences Institute

2-Pc: When entering combat, if the wearer's Max Energy is greater than or equal to 200, increases the wearer's DMG dealt by 0.2% for every 1 point exceeding this value, up to a max increase of 32%.
