# "Pom-Pom's Assist (Beta)": Talk to Pom-Pom to get Pom-Pom's new outfit!
## "Pom-Pom's Assist (Beta)" Feature Description
![Banner](https://sdk.hoyoverse.com/upload/ann/2026/04/17/40805bff4e6ab6364ff105f3e1f99742_7751349668303445703_transformed.png)

The conductor is curious about the Trailblazer's daily routine and wants to help out. Come check out Pom-Pom's recommendations for character progression!

### Availability

After the Version 4.2 update – 2026/05/25 04:00:00 (server)

### Event Requirements

Trailblaze Level ≥ 21 and complete the Trailblaze Mission "Jarilo-VI" - "No Time for Me, My Friend"

### Special Rewards

![img](https://sdk.hoyoverse.com/upload/ann/2026/04/15/5c640a6183daa408951e84e574074aaf_5530941884012894517_transformed.png)

### Details

- In the "Chats" - "Pom-Pom's Assist (Beta)" screen, chat with Pom-Pom a total of 10 times to claim the Pom-Pom Outfit "Age of Trailblaze! Formalwear Set." Starting from the second week (2026/04/27 04:00:00 (server)), Trailblazers can chat with Pom-Pom to claim 50 Stellar Jades every week.

- After the Version 4.3 update, Pom-Pom's Outfit "Age of Trailblaze! Formalwear Set" can be redeemed in Herta's Store using Herta Bonds.

- Trailblazers can ask Pom-Pom about material farming, team lineup recommendations, Relic recommendations, Light Cone recommendations, and other related info.

- On the "Pom-Pom's Assist (Beta)" screen, you can quickly change Pom-Pom's Outfit.

### Note

- During the "Pom-Pom's Assist (Beta)" trial period, Trailblazers are welcome to submit feedback. After 2026/05/25 04:00:00 (server), this feature will be temporarily taken offline for optimization. Please stay tuned to future official announcements for the release date of the official version.
