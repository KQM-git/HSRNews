# Currency Wars & Simulated Universe | Periodic Points Consolidation Details
## Cyclical Points Consolidation Details
![Banner](https://sdk.hoyoverse.com/upload/ann/2026/03/11/582927035035e94e58ab7f217bab08dd_4552117502802085210_transformed.png)

After the Version 4.2 update, the Cyclical Point Rewards for "Currency Wars" and the "Simulated Universe" will be **merged**!

### Point Rewards Adjustment Details

■ After the Version 4.2 update, "Currency Wars" Points and "Simulated Universe" Points will be merged into a new cyclical Point progression track. After the merger, participating in "Currency Wars" or the "Simulated Universe" will earn Points and corresponding Rewards for the new cycle.

- The new cycle for Points updates **once a week**. It refreshes every Monday at 04:00 (server time).

- The **First-Time Completion Rewards** for the original "Divergent Universe" Cyclical Extrapolation will be merged into the Point Rewards of the new cycle. Other reward contents can be referenced from the original "Currency Wars" Point Rewards, including **Deep Immersifiers**, **Fuel Vouchers**, etc.

※ The Simulated Universe includes "Divergent Universe: Arcadian Chronicles" and Simulated Universe series gameplay.

■ New Cycle Point Rewards Preview

![img](https://sdk.hoyoverse.com/upload/ann/2026/03/18/bbb6f9a2e55a3acf1650467eb34e93f7_6998743353414623649_transformed.png)

※ The Point Rewards for the new cycle (Stellar Jades, Star Rail Passes, Tracks of Destiny, Herta Bonds, and Credits) are the same amount as the original "Simulated Universe" and "Currency Wars" Point Rewards.

※ The amount of Fuel Vouchers obtained bi-weekly from the new cycle Points is consistent with the original Point Rewards from "Currency Wars," while the number of Deep Immersifiers is doubled. The new cycle Points will no longer yield Immersifiers.

※ Point consolidation will not affect the Immersifiers already obtained by the Trailblazer. After the consolidation, Immersifiers can still be redeemed by consuming Trailblaze Power.

### Schedule Adjustments Before and After Merge

■ Pre-Points Progression Merge (Version 4.1)

- The calculation of points for the final period of "Currency Wars" in Version 4.1 will **end early**. The cut-off time is 2026/04/22 06:00:00 (server).

- At the end of Version 4.1, the "Simulated Universe" Points scheduled to refresh at 2026/04/20 04:00:00 (global) will **no longer update**. Compensation for this period's rewards will be issued to all eligible Trailblazers via **in-game mail**.

■ Post-Points Progression Merge (Version 4.2)

- After the Version 4.2 update, the new Point cycle will begin immediately. The first period will last for **5 days**, and weekly rotations will resume on the following Monday.
