# "The Fall at Dawn's Rise" Mission Description
## "The Fall at Dawn's Rise" Mission Description
![Banner](https://sdk.hoyoverse.com/upload/ann/2025/04/27/add1b6cf74f3fd2d0c6e67c410602791_3540535401762783599.png)

For a new blazing sun to rise upon the world, the hero must first sever the sky's final eye.

### Availability

Permanently available after the Version 3.3 update

### Unlock Requirement

Complete Trailblaze Mission "Amphoreus — Through the Petals in the Land of Repose"

※ Amphoreus Trailblaze Missions can be experienced in advance through the "Finality's Vision: Leap of Trailblaze" function.
