# Version 3.1 New Stages
## Version 3.1 New Stages
![Banner](https://sdk.hoyoverse.com/upload/ann/2025/02/11/f04b6d68ed439dd227973403c61b54a5_5126428200130786877.png)

Dear Trailblazers,

After Version 3.1 update, new Stages have been added: Divergent Universe: Unceasing Strife, Stagnant Shadow: Shape of Gelidmoon, Stagnant Shadow: Shape of Sloggyre, Calyx (Gold): Amphoreus.

### Planar Ornament Extraction

■ Divergent Universe: Unceasing Strife

Successfully challenging the "Divergent Universe: Unceasing Strife" and use Trailblaze Power or Immersifier to obtain brand new Planar Ornaments **"Bone Collection's Serene Demesne" and "Giant Tree of Rapt Brooding"** in the "Planar Ornament Extraction".

![img](https://sdk.hoyoverse.com/upload/ann/2025/02/17/e80715412b7f332c4942c1ec5992787b_4707405701311712026.png)

New Planar Ornament Effects Preview:

- **"Bone Collection's Serene Demesne"**

Link Rope — Aidonia's Deathward Bone Chains

Planar Sphere — Aidonia's Deceased Gravestones

Two-Piece Set: Increases the wearer's Max HP by 12%. When the wearer's Max HP is 5000 or higher, increases the wearer's and their memosprite's CRIT DMG by 28%.

- **"Giant Tree of Rapt Brooding"**

Link Rope — Grove of Epiphany's Interwoven Veins

Planar Sphere — Grove of Epiphany's Pondering Colossus

Two-Piece Set: Increases the wearer's SPD by 6%. When the wearer's Speed is 135/180 or higher, increases the wearer and their memosprite's Outgoing Healing by 12%/20%.

### Stagnant Shadow

■ Stagnant Shadow: Shape of Gelidmoon

Clear the Stage can obtain the Ascension material "Darkveil Moonlight" for a new Quantum-Type character.

![img](https://sdk.hoyoverse.com/upload/ann/2025/02/17/a69be08f81da225dbeda6f0e2ccfbf87_257237274994822170.png)

■ Stagnant Shadow: Shape of Sloggyre

Clear the Stage to obtain the new Imaginary character's Ascension material "Harbinger of Strife."

![img](https://sdk.hoyoverse.com/upload/ann/2025/02/17/2a3387b8fd336d1fd0d801094dbe547b_3610110018928341226.png)

### Calyx (Golden)

■ Amphoreus

After successfully challenging the "Calyx (Gold)" stages in the Amphoreus region, you can obtain Universal Enhancement Materials and synthesis materials in addition to the original rewards.
